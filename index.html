No login. Nothing leaves your browser. This is an estimate, not a guarantee.

What the pull types mean

  • No pull — they decide without touching your credit report. Zero score impact.
  • Soft pull — they peek at your report to prequalify. Visible only to you, no score impact.
  • Hard pull — a formal application. Can ding your score a few points and stays ~2 years.
